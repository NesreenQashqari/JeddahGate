Bayada Island Snorkeling Trip

Experience seaside relaxation at Bayada Island, a hidden gem known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and soft white sands. Perfect for snorkeling, swimming, and escaping the city, it offers a peaceful retreat surrounded by natural beauty.

Information

Location: Jeddah, Corniche Br Rd, Abhur Al Janoobiyah

Ages: All

Ideal for: Families,Friends, Wheel Chair Accesible

Opening Hours: Daily, 24 hours / 7 days
